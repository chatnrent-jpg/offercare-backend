    <h1 style="visibility: hidden">Maryland registration and tax info</h1>
    <div style="visibility: hidden"><p>Having payroll issues? Learn how to <a href="https://support.gusto.com/article/106621966100000/Troubleshoot-blocked-payroll" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">troubleshoot blocked payroll</a>.</p><h4 id="settinguppayrollinmarylandforthefirsttime">Setting up payroll in Maryland for the first time</h4><p>Before you can pay employees in a new state, you must register with the state’s tax agencies. This lets you file and pay the right taxes.</p><ul><li><p>🎥 <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=60z8-1SYRXg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Watch a quick video</a> to learn how registering in a new state works</p></li><li><p>📘 See our blog for full <a href="https://gusto.com/resources/states" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">state business guides</a></p></li><li><p>🤝 <a href="https://support.gusto.com/article/106622321100000/Maryland-registration-and-tax-info#letusregisterforyou" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Let Gusto register for you</a></p></li></ul><p>Expand the sections below to learn more. Use CMD + F (or CTRL + F) to search for words in the article.</p><div class="expand-collapse" id="rXnNNknNHgvBRzN0kO6I4r" data-anchor="letusregisterforyou">
  <a class="collapsed" href="#" data-toggle="collapse" data-target="#collapse-rXnNNknNHgvBRzN0kO6I4r" aria-expanded="false">

    <span>Let us register for you</span>
  </a>
  <div id="collapse-rXnNNknNHgvBRzN0kO6I4r" class="collapse" style="height: 0px;" aria-expanded="false">
    <p>Gusto works with Middesk to register your business for state tax accounts when you hire employees or start working in a new state. We set up <strong>state income tax withholding</strong> and <strong>state unemployment insurance</strong> so we can run payroll and file taxes on your behalf.</p><p>We can start your registration after all of these are true:</p><ol><li><p>You added a company work address in the new state.</p></li><li><p>You assigned that address to a new or existing employee.</p></li><li><p>You checked your Gusto Home page for a registration to-do.</p></li><li><p>If required, you registered with preliminary state agencies (like the Secretary of State or Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs) first and confirmed it's done.</p></li></ol><p>States that require SOS registration before tax setup: Michigan, Missouri, New Jersey, New Mexico, and Vermont.</p><div class="expand-collapse" id="ODoBtvUEnykMnATKmtdvgK" data-anchor="dl-whocanusethisservice">
  <a class="collapsed" href="#" data-toggle="collapse" data-target="#collapse-ODoBtvUEnykMnATKmtdvgK" aria-expanded="false">
    <em class="icon-arrow-up"></em>
    <em class="icon-arrow-down"></em>
    <span>Check if you’re eligible to use this service</span>
  </a>
  <div id="collapse-ODoBtvUEnykMnATKmtdvgK" class="collapse" style="height: 0px;" aria-expanded="false">
    <p>Middesk cannot help with registration if your business is:</p><ul><li><p>A non-profit that is not a 501(c)(3)</p></li><li><p>A business that left a PEO</p></li><li><p>A business that bought another business that was already registered</p></li><li><p>A business that changed its legal entity type (for example, LLC to Corp)</p></li><li><p>A Limited Partnership (LP) or Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)</p></li></ul><p>You must also:</p><ul><li><p>Have a FEIN in Gusto</p></li><li><p>Not already have a tax account in that state</p></li></ul><p>If you already registered with the state, Middesk will cancel the order and you will not be charged.</p>

In most cases, you must set this up yourself after registration. If you need to register for local taxes or PFML, or set up TPA or POA, use the <a href="https://support.gusto.com/hub/Employers-and-admins/Taxes-forms-and-compliance/State-registration-and-resources" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">state-specific article</a> for step-by-step instructions.</p>

    <p>Here is what to expect when Middesk handles your registration:</p><ol><li><p>You answer questions about your business, including:
</p><ul><li><p>Basic company info</p></li><li><p>Contact details</p></li><li><p>Addresses</p></li><li><p>Owner or officer info</p></li><li><p>State-specific details</p></li></ul></li><li><p>Middesk reviews your information.</p></li><li><p>Middesk usually submits your registration within two business days.</p></li><li><p>A state agency may contact you to verify the request.
</p><ul><li><p>⚠️ <strong>Important:</strong> If you do not respond to a verification call or email within 3–5 business days, the order may be canceled and refunded.</p></li></ul></li><li><p>After your order is marked as complete, we add the charge to your next <strong>Gusto monthly invoice</strong>.</p></li></ol><h4 id="afterregistrationissubmitted">After registration is submitted</h4><p>When Middesk receives your tax account numbers, they enter them in Gusto (for most states and agencies). You will get emails from Gusto and Middesk with updates and next steps.</p><p>For most states, Middesk receives agency mail during registration and adds your tax info to Gusto. You can view this mail in a <strong>digital mailbox</strong>. After registration is complete, the agency updates its mailing address to your business address. If you need to take action, we will notify you by email or <strong>Home</strong> page to-do.</p><p><strong>States that mail tax account info directly to your business:</strong> Alabama, Arizona, Delaware, Idaho, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Maryland, Massachusetts, Michigan, Montana, Nevada, New Hampshire, Oklahoma, Rhode Island, Washington, Washington DC, and West Virginia. When you receive the letter, check your email for instructions on how to send the info to Middesk.</p><h4 id="runningpayrollbeforeregistrationiscomplete">Running payroll before registration is complete</h4><p>You can run payroll before registration finishes. However:</p><ul><li><p>Gusto cannot file or pay taxes until all tax account numbers are added.</p></li><li><p>You may receive late notices or penalties. These are your responsibility.</p></li><li><p>For unemployment tax, use your state’s <a href="https://support.gusto.com/article/106622236100000/State-unemployment-insurance-SUI-tax-for-admins" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">new employer rate</a> until you receive the correct one.</p></li></ul>

    <h4 id="eligibilityandrequirements">Eligibility and requirements</h4><p><strong>Q: Do I need a FEIN to use Gusto’s registration service?</strong></p><p>A: Yes. You must have a FEIN to register through Gusto. If your business is merging or being acquired, Middesk cannot help with registration.</p><p><strong>Q: Why can’t I enter 0% ownership or add a commercial owner?</strong></p><p>A: Some states require a minimum ownership percentage or an individual owner. If your situation does not meet state rules, you will need to register outside of Gusto.</p><p><strong>Q: What if the responsible party is international without an SSN or ITIN?</strong></p><p>A: Middesk cannot help in this case. You will need to register directly with the state.</p><h4 id="unemploymenttax">Unemployment tax</h4><p><strong>Q: What if I’m not liable for unemployment tax yet?</strong></p><p>A: Use today’s date or the expected liability date. Use the <a href="https://support.gusto.com/article/106622236100000/State-unemployment-insurance-SUI-tax-for-admins#SUInewERrates" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">new employer rate</a> until you get your company-specific rate.</p><h4 id="afteryousubmitregistration">After you submit registration</h4><p><strong>Q: I need to change information I already submitted. What do I do?</strong></p><p>A: Email <strong><a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection" class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="0571647d7760626c76717764716c6a6b7645686c616160766e2b666a68">[email&#160;protected]</a></strong>.</p><p><strong>Q: What if the tax agency contacts me?</strong></p><p>A: Respond right away, within 3–5 business days. Missing a verification call or email can cancel your registration order.</p><p><strong>Q: When will my tax account numbers be available?</strong></p><p>A: Timelines vary by state. <a href="https://help.middesk.com/en/categories/1906625" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Check Middesk’s guidance for estimates</a>.</p><h4 id="closingaccounts">Closing accounts</h4><p><strong>Q: Can Middesk close my state tax accounts?</strong></p><p>A: No. You must <a href="https://support.gusto.com/article/221005142111457/Close-or-reopen-a-tax-account" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">close accounts directly with the state</a>.</p>
