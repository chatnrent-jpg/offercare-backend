# Manage W-2 employees

Learn how to manage the onboarding, compensation, benefits, and more for W-2 employees

## Key features

* [**Onboard employees**](https://docs.gusto.com/embedded-payroll/docs/onboard-a-w2-employee): Streamline hiring—including setting up jobs, compensation, and benefits, and ensuring compliance with the completion of required employee forms—with options for [**employee self-onboarding**](https://docs.gusto.com/embedded-payroll/docs/employee-self-management).
* **Manage employees**: Manage an employee's pay and lifecycle with a company, such as managing employee addresses, child support garnishments, recurring reimbursements, and dismissals and rehires. Some additional features:
  * [**Adjust for minimum wage**](https://docs.gusto.com/embedded-payroll/docs/adjust-for-minimum-wage) for hourly employees, depending on their active work address
  * [**Estimate a reasonable salary for S Corp owner-employees**](https://docs.gusto.com/embedded-payroll/docs/calculate-a-reasonable-salary-for-s-corp-owner-employees) based on BLS data, to help ensure IRS compliance
